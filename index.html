About NMOSD

Autoimmune Rare Disease

NMO is an autoimmune rare disease that affects the optic nerve and spinal cord.

Common Among Women

NMO is more common among women, affecting them three times more than men.

Symptoms

Symptoms include blurry vision, weakness, numbness, sleeping problems, and more.
